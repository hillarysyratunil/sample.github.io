To God


Dear God,

I thank You for all the blessings that You have showered upon me throughout all these years. Thank You for being the one that is always there for me, answering my prayers, listening to my cries, and blessing me with your unconditional love. When I felt like I was alone and needed a friend, You were there for me. When I could not walk anymore, You were there and You carried me. Most of all, thank You for creating me and giving me the gift of life.

Thank You for giving me strength when I had none. For helping me every time I have a big decision to make. For giving me courage and bravery as I face through each day.

Thank You for giving me the gift of family and friends. For giving me people who love me the way they do. Thank You for all of the people You have allowed me to meet along my journey through life.

Lord, everything I do, I do it all for You. I love You in my weakness and through my strength; may You help me love You even more.


From the Bottom of My Heart,

Hillary
