To My Family and Friends


Dear Family and Friends,


Thank you for always believing in me more than I believe in myself and supporting me in all that I do. Thank you for loving me for me and not as someone who I am not. Thank you for being there for me, through the good times and bad. For comforting me and being a shoulder to cry on when I am sad. Most of all, for all the years of laughter and unforgettable memories.

You inspire me to work hard every day. You make me want to strive to be a better person. You trust me and I trust you too. You give me reasons to smile even if all I want to do is cry. You taught me to never settle anything less than what I deserve. You showed me what it means to be loved and how to love in return.

I wish you could see yourself the way I see you. I wish you could love yourself the way I love you. Above all, I wish your life is everything you deserve because no one deserves the world more than you. Who I am today, I owe it all to you and for that I am eternally grateful. I hope you know that I will always have so much love in my heart for you.


From the Bottom of My Heart,

Hillary
