To Myself


Dear Self,


I may not say this often enough but I am proud of you. I am proud of you for trying your best every day. I know that it is not easy but do not ever give up. It will all be worth it in the end. Remember to take care of yourself. To eat when you are hungry, to drink when you are thirsty, to rest when you are tired, and to take time to breathe.

You are an inspiration. Do not forget to take a moment and see how far you have come. Always remember that where you are is exactly where you are supposed to be. And on the days that you feel like giving up, always remember your why. Do not forget the reason why you are doing this in the first place.

I admire how you dream big. How you strive for what you truly deserve and never settle for anything less because you deserve the world. Continue to do what you are passionate about and live your dreams, I will be there for you every step of the way. Always remember that there are people who love you and care for you dearly. You are not alone.

Always remember how much I love you and that I am always here for you.


From the Bottom of My Heart,

Hillary
